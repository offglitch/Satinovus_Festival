About

Sattinovus (Sati is “to remember” in Sanskrit, Novus is “new” in latin) is a festival that celebrates innovation in technology and arts while also gathering creative minds to showcase their projects and exchange knowledge.

Objective

Each year, we set an objective with a message to share with the world.
This year, our focus is geared towards inclusive and accessible technology. Think technology that is not only accessible by everyone, but can also be used by everyone. Inclusive technology is needed today more than ever as technology becomes the defining mean for how humans go about their everyday lives. Can everyone use your app seamlessly? Is your service accessible by individuals from different geolocations? These are the types of questions we’re trying to find answers to this year!
